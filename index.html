Running as a Hobby

Why I do it

For high school, I had to move to a new city where I didn't know anyone. In order to make friends and feel involved at school, I joined the cross country team. It was hard work at first, since at the time I wasn't in very good shape, but after four years I came to love it and it's something I continue to do this day.

The benefits of Running

I won't claim to be some fitness trainer that can explain exactly why you should you run, but I can tell you what I've seen as helpful for me. Firstly, it helps me keep in shape. I feel physically good and it helps me with my job. It also helps with my mental health, and I often run before I know I need to focus for a while.
